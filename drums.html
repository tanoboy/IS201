I have been drumming since the seventh grade. My middle school required all students to take an art credit. I chose percussion, and it changed my life forever.

  1. Drums are the most entertaining instrument
    • Visually, watching drums is more entertaining than any other instrument.
    • Not only is it fun for the audience, it's a blast for the musician.
  2. Drumming burns calories
    • An hour of drum set playing burns around the same amount of calories as walking 10,000 steps.
    • Moving your arms and legs at fast speeds is a great way to get exercise.
  3. Drumming is used in almost every genre of music
    • Rock, Jazz, Metal, Pop, Indie, you name it.
    • Modern drumming combines technical drumming with drumless tracks, highlighting the skills of the drummer.
